The Problem

Campus taught you theory.
The job market wants skills.

Thousands of Kenyan graduates leave university every year with a certificate and no practical skill that earns. The gap between classroom and career is real.

  • You studied for 4 years but cannot build, code, analyse data, or market a product.
  • Internships require experience you do not have yet which is a cruel cycle blocking fresh graduates.
  • Online courses from the West cost USD and teach for a market that does not match Kenya.
  • The gig economy is open but nobody showed you how to position, find clients, or price your work.

Do I need prior tech experience?
No. We teach from absolute ground zero. If you can use a smartphone, you have enough starting knowledge for any of our courses.
How is course content delivered?
Course materials are hosted on Drive with conditional access unlocked on enrollment confirmation. Full courses have live cohort sessions. Crash courses are fully self-paced.
How do I pay?
Payment is via M-Pesa. Details provided at enrollment. Installment arrangements are available. Reach out via WhatsApp if you need a payment plan.
What is Tumakazi Jobtech?
Tumakazi Jobtech (tumakazi.com) is our partner freelance platform. After completing your course you are onboarded and actively supported in finding gigs that match your new skill set.
